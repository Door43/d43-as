===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Will you judge them? Will you judge, Son of man? ****Let them know their fathers’ abominations **- These rhetorical questions introduce a new topic: God wants Ezekiel to let the descendants of Israel know of their ancestors' sins. (See: Rheorical question)
  * **made myself known…them **- God started a relationship with…. the Israelites.
  * **flowed with milk and honey**  - The land is fertile and beautiful.
